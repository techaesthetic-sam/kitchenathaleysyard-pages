Delete Your Account

At Kitchen at Haley’s Yard, we respect your privacy and give you full control over your personal data. You can delete your account and all associated data directly within the app at any time.

How to Delete Your Account

  • Open the Kitchen at Haley’s Yard app on your device.
  • Go to the Profile section.
  • Tap Delete Account.
  • Confirm your choice. Once confirmed, your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted from our servers.

Deletion requests are processed immediately and completed within 7 business days. After deletion, your account cannot be recovered.
